    Call Volume Forecast



    Call Volume Forecasting - The process of predicting the number of inbound contacts (calls, chats, emails, etc.) expected to arrive in a given time interval, based on historical data, patterns, and known events, so staffing can be planned to meet service goals.



    Core Elements:

    • Historical Data Analysis - identifying recurring patterns in volume by day of week, time of day, season, etc.
    • Trend Adjustments - Accounting for long-term growth/decline in contact demand.
    • Event Impacts - Factoring in promotions, product launches, outages, holidays, or campaignes.
    • Outlier Treatment - Removing or adjusting for abnormal spikes/dips that don't reflect normal conditions.
    • Interval Forecasting - Breaking the forecast down to 15- or 30-minute intervals for scheduling accuracy
