Realtime Analyst



Realtime Analyst (RTA) - monitors and manages live operations to ensure service levels and efficiency by tracking call volume, agent availability, and schedule adherence. They make real-time adjustments to staffing, workloads, and schedules using workforce management software to respond to unexpected events like high call volumes or agent absenteeism. Key responsibilities include analyzing live data, communicating with supervisors, ensuring optimal resource allocation, and making quick, data-driven decisions to maintain performance goals.
