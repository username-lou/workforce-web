Average Speed of Answer



Average Speed of Answer (ASA) - A metric that measures the percentage of customer issues resolved in the first interaction, without the need for the customer to call back, be transferred, or have the case escalated.



Formula:

    ASA   =      Total Wait Time of Answered Calls    x100

    Total Number of Answered Calls
